About Florida Roof Marketplace

Connecting Clients with Reliable Roof Repair Service Providers

Florida Roof Marketplace is an online platform designed to connect clients in need of roofing services with reliable service providers who can fulfill those requests. Our goal is to simplify the process of finding and hiring professionals for your roofing needs.

Whether you have a leaking roof, missing or damaged shingles, sagging roof, issues with flashing, valleys, low slope or poor roof pitch, gutter and downspout problems, fascia and soffit damage, roof truss issues, Florida Roof Marketplace is here to connect you with professionals to assist you.

With our user-friendly website, clients can easily post their service requests and specify the type of roofing work they need. Professional Service providers registered on our platform can then browse through the requests and offer their services to fulfill those needs.

  • Quick and Convenient: Easily post your roof repair service requests.
  • Connect with trustworthy and experienced professionals.
  • We offer Wide Range of Services and solutions for all roofing needs.
  • With our Simplified Process and Streamlined platform we provide efficient communication and transactions.
  • With our professional providers we offer high-quality workmanship and customer satisfaction.

Missing or Damaged Shingle Replacement

We specialize in all types of roof systems.

Asphalt Shingles, Metal Roof, Tile Roofs, Flat Roofs, Slate Roofs, TPO and much more.

Flashing Repair

Repair and replacement of damaged flashing to prevent water intrusion and maintain a watertight roof system.

Valley Repair

Expert repair of valleys, where two roof planes meet, to prevent water penetration and ensure proper drainage.

Low Slope or Poor Roof Pitch Repair

Specialized solutions to address low slope or poor roof pitch issues, improving water runoff and preventing leaks.

Gutter and Downspout Repair

Repair and maintenance of gutters and downspouts to ensure proper drainage and prevent water damage.

Fascia and Soffit Repair

Repair and replacement of damaged fascia and soffit boards, enhancing the aesthetics and protection of your roof.

Roof Truss Repair

Specialized repair and reinforcement of roof trusses to ensure the structural integrity of your roof system.
